I work at the intersection of operations, workforce analytics, and business performance, using data to understand how teams and systems operate and where improvements can be made.

I’ve built dashboards, automated reporting, and worked closely with Operations, Training, and Workforce Management teams to identify gaps, improve workflows, and turn reporting into something teams actively use.

  • Increased utilization to 95%
  • Reduced idle time to 3%
  • Improved reporting turnaround by 20% through automation
  • Delivered up to 25% improvement in team productivity

I primarily work with SQL, Python, and Power BI, but the focus is always on making data practical, usable, and tied to real business outcomes.

While much of my experience comes from operations-focused environments, my approach applies across industries where data can improve efficiency, performance, and decision-making.
